# RakriAI — Facebook Business Page Setup Guide

## Why This Matters

When you message a UK plumber on Facebook, the FIRST thing they do is click your profile. If they see an empty page with 0 followers and no posts — they ignore you. If they see a professional page with work samples, testimonials, and a clear pitch — they respond.

**Goal:** Make your Facebook page look like an established web design agency within 24 hours, starting from zero.

---

## PHASE 1: Create the Page (15 minutes)

### Step 1: Create the page
1. Go to https://www.facebook.com/pages/create
2. Choose **"Business or Brand"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Page name:** `RakriAI — Websites for Tradespeople`
   - **Category:** Search and select `Web Designer` (also add `Marketing Agency`)
   - **Bio:** `We build professional websites for plumbers, electricians, roofers & more. See live demos at demo.rakriai.com`

### Step 2: Page info (do this immediately)
Go to **Page Settings → Page Info** and fill EVERYTHING:

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| **Username** | `@rakriai` (this gives you facebook.com/rakriai) |
| **Website** | `https://rakriai.com` |
| **Email** | `connect@rakriai.com` |
| **Phone** | Your WhatsApp Business number |
| **Hours** | Mon-Fri 9AM-6PM GMT |
| **About** | "We build professional, mobile-friendly websites for local service businesses — plumbers, electricians, roofers, pest control, HVAC. Based in India, serving the UK, UAE & Europe. See our live demos: demo.rakriai.com" |
| **Impressum** | `RakriAI | Web Design for Local Businesses | connect@rakriai.com` |

### Step 3: Profile picture
- Use a clean, professional logo
- Option A: Go to https://www.canva.com → search "web design logo" → customize with "RakriAI" text
- Option B: Use text-based logo — dark background, white text "RakriAI" in a modern font
- Size: 170×170px minimum
- **Keep it simple** — a clean wordmark looks more professional than a complex icon

### Step 4: Cover photo
- Size: 820×312px
- Create on Canva → search "web design agency cover photo"
- Must include:
  - "Professional Websites for Local Businesses"
  - "demo.rakriai.com"
  - Show mockup screenshots of your demo sites (take screenshots of your plumber/electrician/roofer demo pages)
  - Clean, dark/modern aesthetic matching your demo sites
- **This is the first thing people see — make it count**

## PHASE 3: Build Credibility Fast (30 minutes)

### Get your first followers (before outreaching)
You need at least 20-50 followers so it doesn't look dead.

1. **Invite your personal friends** — Go to Page → Invite Friends → invite 50-100 people. Even if 20 accept, that's enough.

2. **Join 5-10 Facebook Groups:**
   - "UK Small Business Owners"
   - "Small Business UK"
   - "UK Tradespeople Network"
   - "Plumbers UK"
   - "Self Employed UK"

   Share helpful content (NOT selling) — answer questions about websites, share tips. Add your page link to your profile.

3. **Like and comment** on other business pages in your niche. When you comment as your page (not personal profile), people see your page name.

4. **Cross-post to Instagram** — Connect your Instagram to your Facebook page. Same posts go to both platforms. Doubles your reach.

### Get your first reviews
1. Ask 3-5 friends/family to leave a 5-star review on your page
2. Review text examples:
   - "Himanshu built our company website in 3 days. Professional, fast, great communication. Highly recommend!"
   - "Amazing work for the price. Our website looks like it cost thousands but was very affordable."
   - "Very responsive and easy to work with. The website is beautiful and already getting us calls."

## PHASE 4: Profile Optimization (15 minutes)

### Action button
Go to Page → Add Button → choose **"Send Message"**
This makes it one-click for leads to message you.

### Auto-reply (IMPORTANT)
Go to Page → Inbox → Automated Responses → Instant Reply:

```
Hi! Thanks for reaching out to RakriAI 👋

I'm Himanshu — I build professional websites for local businesses
like yours.

I'll reply within a few hours. In the meantime, you can see
live demos of our work at: demo.rakriai.com

Looking forward to chatting!
```

### Page tabs
Keep only relevant tabs visible:
- ✅ Home
- ✅ About
- ✅ Photos
- ✅ Reviews
- ✅ Videos (if you make any)

## PHASE 6: What Your Page Should Look Like Before Outreaching

### Checklist — DO NOT message anyone until ALL boxes are checked:

```
□ Profile picture (clean logo)
□ Cover photo (shows your work + demo.rakriai.com)
□ Username set (@rakriai)
□ All page info filled (website, email, hours, about)
□ At least 10 posts published
□ At least 3 of those posts show demo website screenshots
□ At least 20 followers (invite friends)
□ At least 3 five-star reviews
□ Auto-reply message set up
□ "Send Message" button active
□ Page looks active (posts spread over 3-4 days, not all on same day)
```

### The test:
Open your page in an incognito browser. Look at it as a stranger would. Ask yourself: "Would I trust this business to build my website?"

If yes → start outreaching.
If no → fix what's missing.
