## PHASE 1: Create the Page (15 minutes)

### Step 1: Create the page
1. Go to https://www.facebook.com/pages/create
2. Choose **"Business or Brand"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Page name:** `Rakri AI`
   - **Category:** Select `Software Company` (also add `Web Designer`, `AI Company`)
   - **Bio:** `Engineers Building AI You Own. Custom AI systems for startups & enterprises. Professional websites for local businesses. rakriai.com`

### Step 2: Page info — fill EVERYTHING

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| **Username** | `@rakriai` (gives you facebook.com/rakriai) |
| **Website** | `https://rakriai.com` |
| **Email** | `connect@rakriai.com` |
| **Phone** | Your WhatsApp Business number |
| **Hours** | Mon-Fri 9AM-6PM GMT |
| **About** | "Rakri AI — Engineers Building AI You Own. We build custom AI systems (private models, SaaS platforms, workflow automation) for startups and enterprises. We also build professional websites for local service businesses. 100% client-owned. Zero vendor lock-in. rakriai.com" |
| **Founded** | 2024 |
| **Products** | Custom AI Solutions, Private AI Models, AI SaaS Development, Workflow Automation, Professional Websites |

### Step 3: Profile picture
- Rakri AI logo on dark background
- Size: 170×170px
